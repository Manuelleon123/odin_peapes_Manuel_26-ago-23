Cachitos

Description

Cachitos are a Venezuelan food similar to the croissant, and are often filled with ham and cheese.

ingredients

  • 1 cup Warm Water
  • 1 No.s pack active dry yeast
  • 4 tablespoon Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 3 cup Flour
  • 1 tablespoon flour for dusting
  • 600 gram Ham
  • 300 gram bacon

Steps

  1. in a bowl take flour and add a mixture of warm water, sugar and yeast to it.
  2. add butter and salt to the bowl and combine everything together.
  3. knead it into a dough and set aside for 40 minutes.
  4. heat oil in a pan and saute ham and bacon in it.
  5. flatten the dough and cut it into triangular pieces.
  6. spread ham-bacon mix on top of the rolled dough and roll it to make cachitos
  7. place them on a baking tray and bake for 15 minutes at 375 degree f.
